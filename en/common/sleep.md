> Delay for a specified amount of time.
> More information: <https://www.gnu.org/software/coreutils/sleep>.

#### Delay in seconds:
```shell
sleep {{seconds}}
```
#### Delay in minutes:
```shell
sleep {{minutes}}m
```
#### Delay in hours:
```shell
sleep {{hours}}h
```
